I'm a brand marketer who has spent seven years proving that the best marketing doesn't announce itself. It shows up as a moment someone remembers, a campaign they talk about, a brand that earns its place in their life.

I hold a Master of Global Management (Marketing Specialization) from Royal Roads University, and I'm currently based in Montreal, open to senior brand and marketing leadership roles across Canada.
